# PROJECT.md

## Loyiha Nomi

NEXUS ERP CRM HR AI PLATFORM

## Maqsad

Katta bizneslar, korporatsiyalar, IT kompaniyalar, zavodlar va xizmat ko‘rsatish firmalari uchun ERP, CRM, HRM, Finance, Inventory, Recruitment, Analytics, Monitoring va AI Automation tizimlarini yagona platformada birlashtirish.

## Arxitektura

- Enterprise Modular Architecture
- API-first
- Microservice-ready module boundaries
- Multi-tenant ready
- RBAC/RLS-ready authorization model
- Realtime-ready event layer
- Cloud-native deployment path

## Asosiy Modullar

- ERP: finance, inventory, procurement, reporting
- CRM: clients, companies, pipeline, communications
- HRM: employees, departments, attendance, payroll, leave
- Recruitment: vacancies, CV uploads, AI resume analysis, interviews
- AI: assistants, analytics, automation, recommendations
- Analytics: KPI, forecasting, heatmaps, realtime charts
- Security: audit logs, session tracking, device history, login alerts

## Enterprise Standart

Platforma scalable, modular, maintainable, enterprise-ready va production deployment uchun tayyorlanadi.
